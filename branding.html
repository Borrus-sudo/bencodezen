Bio

Long Bio

With a non-traditional background in I/O Psychology, Ben is passionate about building products that are a joy for users to interact with. He has worked across a variety of projects including NASA Earthdata, Healthcare.gov (as part of the team to help fix the site after its initial launch) and Medicare.gov.

In conjunction with his pursuit of improving his understanding of instructional design, he enjoys teaching and mentoring people in the field. He can often be found at local events involving technology, design, entrepreneurship, and the startup community. In addition, he is also an avid Go (strategy game) fan who loves great food, Kali-Eskrima and hiking.

Short Bio

Ben is a front-end developer who is passionate about building products designed with user experience and interaction design at its core.
