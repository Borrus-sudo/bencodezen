Stringendo Studios

Simply the best school for strings

Welcome to Stringendo Studios! With 1000 students passing through our doors each week, you can be assured that we have the experience to provide top notch training.

About the Studio

Our eminent faculty are professional musicans, and most of them are members of the Anytown Orchestra.

Stringendo Studios is open to students of all ages and levels of ability with classes for young children through senior citizens. Stringendo Studios provides numerous opportunities for all students to perform and make music with each other.

Support Classes

We also offer classes in Music Theory, Keyboard Skills, Ear Training, Sinfonia, Orchestra, and Music History to support the core curriculum for the serious string student. You can choose a comprehensive musicianship package when you sign you or your child up for private lessons. Read more on our classes page.

Competition Winners

Our students win many local, national, and international competitions. Many also go on to study at the world's most prestigious institutions, such as Peabody Conservatory, The Jacobs School of Music of Indiana University, Curtis, Eastman, and Juilliard. You can read the latest news about our student competition winners and Stringendo Studios graduates at our student news page.
